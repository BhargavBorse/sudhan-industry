Step Lap Lamination Type

Transformer core lamination for Power & Distribution transformer is manufactured from various grades of Cold Rolled Grain Oriented (CRGO) steel. Lamination in various shapes & sizes are slit and cut to required accurate geometrical shapes with 90° & 45° cutting systems. The manufacturing of Transformer Lamination involves a high degree of precision engineering.

CRGO Transformer Lamination Core

As we receive design from our customer we study and check design dimensions no load loss and suitable grade as per customer requirement and as per the design we take out Samples from mother coils if the no load loss is match with the customer requirement the same coil converted into process of slitting / cutting / “V” Noching and Punching as per customer requirement.
