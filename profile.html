Quality and Management policy

A quality management system (QMS) is a set of policies, processes and procedures required for planning and execution (production/development/service) in the core business area of an organization. Our company Sudhan Industries integrates the various internal processes within the organization and intends to provide a process approach. A process based QMS enables the organizations to identify, measure, control and improve the various core business processes that will ultimately lead to improved business performance.
